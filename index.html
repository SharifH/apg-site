Frequently asked Questions

What do I need to get started?

You do not need to know your diagnosis or treatment goals to get in touch-this is often a big part of our initial work together. All you have to know is what in your life is concerning or confusing to you. Get in touch and well gladly provide a brief, initial phone consultation to address any questions you might have.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept a variety of different Insurance Plans to help meet the needs of our patients. We also will meet patients without insurance.

Will I get reimbursed?

It is our experience that most patients are reimbursed for 50 to 80% for the cost of their sessions if they have out-of-network mental health coverage. It is helpful to call individual insurance providers to determine level of coverage in advance. Most are pleasantly surprised.

How does the reimbursement process work

At the end of each month, a statement of services will be provided to you in person or via email. This form should be submitted to your insurance company, along with a health insurance claim form, to receive payment. Most insurance carriers process each claim like any other medical claim. Should any questions arise about services, please feel free to have them contact your therapist directly.
